  • Heroes of Warland

    Heroes of Warland is an online free-to-play mobile FPS, where I worked mainly on client-side code. My work did also include creating some server-side functionalities

  • Medals of War

    Medals of War is an online free-to-play strategy game, where I worked creating major front-end features. My thesis work was done for Medals of War as well.

    Runegate Heroes is an online MMOBA made in cooperation with Wargaming. In Runegate Heroes I was responsible for creating new front-end features.

  • Space Out!

    Space Out! is a resource management game made with a couple friends for a Ludum Dare game jam. I was responsible for creating planet a spawning algorithm and an upgrade tree.
